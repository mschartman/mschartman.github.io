AppFolio
Matt started working at AppFolio in June 2013. He was on the team that built the online leases feature, allowing property managers to create their leases online and send them to tenants for electronic signing. He is currently helping to expand the payments platform and add features for student housing.

Receipt.com
Matt worked at Receipt.com from 2011-2013. During much of his time there, he was the primary maintainer of the Python based receipt interception software that ran on thousands of point of sale systems. He helped build the receipt parsing system that handled dozens of different restaurants and retailers. Also, he helped scale the backend infrastructure that grew to process over a million transactions a day.

Other
Matt has also worked on coding and architecting startup projects involving everything from online food purchase and delivery to building a platform-as-a-service.
